Delete User Account
If you wish to delete your Corral account, please follow these steps:

1. Send an Email
To initiate the account deletion process, please send an email from the email address associated with your account to support@startcorral.com with the subject line "Delete Account Request." In the email, include the following information:

  • Your account username
  • A brief explanation of your request

2. Confirm Your Identity
For security purposes, you may be required to verify your identity before we can process your request. This may involve answering security questions or providing additional information.

3. Account Deletion
Once your identity is confirmed, we will proceed with deleting your account and all associated data. You will receive a confirmation email once the process is complete.

Please note that account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. If you have any questions or need assistance, feel free to contact our support team.
